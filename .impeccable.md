## Design Context

### Users
DeFi traders on FX + perpetual futures. Two co-existing personas:
- **Serious traders**: come in with intent (open/close a position, manage risk on a chart). Want chart-first, dense info, fast order entry, persistent Long/Short CTAs. Their context is often mobile during the day (in transit, at a coffee shop, peeking during work) or at a desk with multiple monitors. They benchmark mentally against Binance/Hyperliquid/Coinbase Advanced.
- **Casual users**: explore via the Arcade gamified mode, leaderboard ("copy trade @kawaii_whale"), and small lend/borrow positions. Want playful surfaces, low cognitive load, friendly framing of risk.

Mobile is utility-first for the serious persona. Casual surfaces (Arcade, Leaderboard) keep more brand expression.

### Brand Personality
**Kawaii fintech.** Three words: **playful, soft, distinct**.
- Mascot: BU ghost ("boofi") — appears in skeletons, empty states, header.
- Voice: friendly without being childish. "Park your dollars to earn yield" not "Maximize APY on stablecoin deposits."
- Emojis used liberally on leaderboard names (`@kawaii_whale`, `@zen_trader_42`, `@mint_arbitrage`), chip selectors (🌸💛🍯✦🍃✨), flag pairs on every market.
- Display font: **knicknack** (rounded, soft, distinctive — already loaded). Body: monospace `JetBrains Mono` for numeric data, neutral sans for labels.

Anti-references: Hyperliquid (too dark/serious), generic dark glassmorphism DEX UIs, anything that feels like a Bybit clone.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme: light primary, dark supported.** Trading happens in many contexts — soft light surfaces feel less aggressive than dark + neon. Dark mode is available as a preference toggle.
- **Color palette**: purple primary (`#6954CF` purpleDanis), soft lavender surface (`#EDE9FE`), violet accent (`#CBB0FF`). Profit = **lavender purple** `--profit: #a89ce8` (not green — deliberate brand inversion). Loss = **soft yellow/pink** `--loss: #ffecb4` (not red). This is a kawaii-fintech tell: signaling positive/negative without the harsh green/red dopamine loop.
- **Tint neutrals toward purple** (chroma 0.005-0.01). Already done in palette.
- **Spacing**: 4pt scale. Density tighter on mobile Trade (info density wins) and looser on Arcade/Leaderboard (breathing room reinforces play).
- **Motion**: framer-motion with iOS-style easing already applied in mobile menu. Stagger reveals on dock items. No bounce/elastic.

### Design Principles (apply to all future work)
1. **On mobile, density serves the trade.** Chart + CTAs must be reachable thumb-first. Order Book / Trades / Depth become tabs, not always-visible columns. Sticky Long/Short.
2. **Brand is a topping, not the base.** Trading surfaces (Trade, Positions) get sober chrome with kawaii accents (ghost mascot in empty states, emoji avatars). Casual surfaces (Arcade, Leaderboard) get full kawaii treatment.
3. **Profit/loss colors stay purple/yellow, never green/red.** This is the most distinctive brand decision — preserve it everywhere.
4. **Numbers are monospace; labels are sans; titles are knicknack.** Three-font hierarchy already established — apply consistently.
5. **No side-stripe borders, no gradient text, no glassmorphism for decoration.** Reserved bans from impeccable. Use full borders, weight, and rhythm instead.
6. **Reach test**: every action a trader takes more than once on mobile (Long, Short, switch market, change size) must be reachable within 1 thumb-tap from the chart. If it takes 2+ taps, reconsider.

### Reference points (not copy-from)
- Binance mobile: chart-first, tabbed orderbook, sticky CTAs — borrow the structural pattern.
- Hyperliquid: dense info density per row — borrow the data formatting, NOT the dark visual.
- Robinhood: motion polish on numbers, soft palette — borrow the touch.
